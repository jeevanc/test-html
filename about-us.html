About Us

Introduction

TimroHamroGhar.com is the only website in Nepal to provide the information about apartments,room,falt,condos etc. for those people who are looking to rent and those who are giving them on rent inside Kathmadnu valley. Evey year,roughly 10,000+ people come to Kathmandu looking for opportunities and most of them are students and single people. Searching flats or rooms by sitting in one place can be a far better way than walking door-to-door of every house and apartments.With the help of this website,searching can be done easily and comfortably which saves the time as every second is precious in today's world.

Our Mission

  • To provide online information and services for those who are looking for rent.
  • To provide online information and services for those who are looking to rent.
  • To help those people who are coming out of Kathmandu valley and looking for rooms.
